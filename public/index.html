Welcome

Your Home in the Mountains

Experience Norwegian Mountain Living

Nestled in the dramatic valley of Rjukan, our vacation home offers the perfect base for your Norwegian adventure. Whether you're seeking winter thrills on the slopes or summer hikes through pristine wilderness, you'll find comfort and convenience in every season.

The house combines modern amenities with mountain charm, providing a cozy retreat after days spent exploring the stunning landscapes that made Rjukan famous. Wake up to mountain views, enjoy the nearby facilities, and immerse yourself in authentic Norwegian nature.

Located in a peaceful neighborhood with easy access to Rjukan's attractions, our home welcomes you to experience the unique character of this historic industrial town surrounded by towering peaks and cascading waterfalls.
